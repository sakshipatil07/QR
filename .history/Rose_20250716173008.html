गुलाब / Rose

Scientific Name: Rosa indica

Benefits:

  • Soothes skin and treats acne
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Enhances mood and reduces stress
  • Improves hydration and skin tone

How to Take Care:

  • Requires 5-6 hours of sunlight daily
  • Water 2–3 times a week (avoid overwatering)
  • Prune dead flowers and stems regularly
  • Use fertilizer once a month
