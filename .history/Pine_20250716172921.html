चिद / Pine Tree

Scientific Name: Pinus longifolia

Benefits:

  • Used in aromatherapy for stress relief
  • Anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial
  • Supports respiratory health
  • Rich in antioxidants

How to Take Care:

  • Plant in well-drained soil
  • Requires full sunlight (6+ hours)
  • Water moderately, let soil dry between watering
  • No need for frequent pruning
